Popular Tourist Places

Dainthlen Falls

The Dainthlen waterfalls are located in the district of East Khasi Hills, Meghalaya. Just before reaching Sohra, a road to the right, leads one to the falls which is 5 kms away.

Rainbow Falls

Rainbow Falls is an enthralling waterfall that lies in the beautiful village of Nongriat near Cherrapunji, Meghalaya, India.

Echo park

Eco Parks are sustainable, environmentally responsive green spaces designed to preserve biodiversity, offer eco-tourism, and promote environmental education.

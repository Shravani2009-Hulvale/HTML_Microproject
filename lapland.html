About Lapland

Lapland is a magical region in northern Finland known for its snowy landscapes, Northern Lights, and unique Arctic experiences. It is famous as the home of Santa Claus and offers activities like husky sledding, reindeer safaris, and ice hotels.

Top Tourist Attractions in Lapland

Northern Lights

Experience the breathtaking Aurora Borealis lighting up the Arctic sky.

Santa Claus Village

Meet Santa and cross the Arctic Circle in this magical destination.

Levi Ski Resort

A popular destination for skiing, snowboarding, and winter adventures.

About Manali

Manali is a popular hill station located in Himachal Pradesh, India. It is known for its snow-covered mountains, scenic valleys, adventure sports, and cool climate. Tourists visit Manali for trekking, skiing, river rafting, and peaceful nature experiences.

Top Tourist Attractions in Manali

Solang Valley

A famous destination for adventure sports like paragliding, skiing, and zorbing.

Rohtang Pass

A high mountain pass offering snow activities and stunning Himalayan views.

Hadimba Temple

An ancient wooden temple surrounded by cedar forests.
